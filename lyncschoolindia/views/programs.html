Lync School Program Description

Our training programs are designed to accommodate students of all levels. A student is thoroughly interviewed by a panel of interviewers. They are then analyzed and guided accordingly. They will be placed in an appropriate program and technology of their choice according to their capabilities.

Are you confused of what technology to choose? We interview, analyze, guide and help you choose a career technology path.

  • We have round the clock support

Lync School accommodates students of all levels in the three training programs that it offers; Rookie, Sophomore and Professional.

Rookie Program

Beginners with little knowledge on technology and have good communication skills will be placed in our Rookie program. Students gain elaborate knowledge on technology in three phases. The first phase covers the computer basics, second covers the intermediate concepts and the third covers the advanced. To ensure complete training, pre-requisites have to be completed before the advanced topics are covered.We have built world class software engineering curriculum and programming courses
